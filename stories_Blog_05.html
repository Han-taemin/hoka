JAN 31, 2023Introducing Transport with Hy Rosario

We sat down with Director of Footwear, Hy Rosario, to get the scoop on the all-new adventure and commuter shoe at HOKA: Transport! Continue reading to learn more about the innovation that went into this versatile shoe's design.

  • HOKA: Introduce yourself!

    Hy Rosario: My name is Hy Rosario, and I'm the Director of Outdoor Footwear at HOKA. I joined the HOKA team in 2019, and now I'm thrilled to introduce the Transport, our newest, most versatile shoe.

    HOKA: What was the inspiration behind this new shoe?

    Hy: Transport was the result of deep research into the shifting needs of our consumer during and after the pandemic. During this time, we all found new ways to move and achieve our step and sweat goals. As HOKA fans began to ponder the “new norm,” they were in search of alternate ways to travel, commute back to work, and continue to lead active lives and they wanted to do it all with the HOKA comfort they discovered during the pandemic. An overwhelming majority of consumers were not satisfied with their current commuter shoes. There was an unmet need for products that could seamlessly move from the trail to city commuting.

  • HOKA: What makes Transport different?

    Hy: Our product creation team which includes designers, developers, colorists, insights teams, and product managers worked for over two years to deep dive into the rapidly evolving needs of our customers. The result is a shoe that brings a high level of performance wrapped in a versatile design. We coined the term “stealth performance” to capture this ethos and build a product that was trail-worthy and city-proof. Transport was wear-tested rigorously and scored for design aesthetics such as materials, colors and finishes to meet the versatility requirement. All the things you love about HOKA, comfort, durability and performance, are now built into this crossover style. Let the adventures begin!

    HOKA: What are you most proud of about Transport?

    Hy: Our team worked diligently to blend high-performance elements such as a Vibram outsole, Cordura mesh, Sugarcane-based EVA, 360-degrees of reflectivity, and adjustability bringing it all together in a way that can move fluidly from the trail to your work commute. We are especially proud of the extra effort applied to engineer a new level of consideration for sustainability, including applying rPET yarns, Vibram EcoStep Natural rubber with 90% less petro chemicals, and an insole made with 50% soy-based PU.

    A major shoutout to Gabe Nicasio, HOKA Designer, and Ryan Ritzau and Jamie Cannady, both HOKA Developers, for their passion and commitment to delivering something truly special. Their research gave us a new window into future approaches for a new class of products. You are going to love the Transport as much as we loved working to build it!

  • HOKA: Who is the shoe for?

    Hy: Transport is for anyone who loves the HOKA performance experience they get from their running, lifestyle, or hiking shoes. They are most likely city dwellers who prefer understated styling without compromising on key performance features. Outdoor escapes are something they love but find most footwear to have limited wearing opportunities beyond the trail. Their active lifestyle requires practical products that can withstand the city and the trail.

    For example, we designed Transport to include an easy-to-adjust toggle closure so it would be more bike-friendly. (Not to worry, if you don't like toggles, we've included an extra lace to keep it analog.) Low-light conditions during autumn and winter can be a hazard for commuters, so we added 360-degrees of reflectivity. And we applied a DWR treatment which is PFC-free/PFAS-free to keep your shoes easy to clean (like an invisible shield that keeps ketchup or your favorite beverage from adhering).

    These additional performance features delivered a modern aesthetic and put an exclamation point on versatility. We are excited to see what new adventures your Transport shoes will inspire, whether on the trail or on the pavement. Get ready to adventure, everywhere!

    Check out the all-new Transport, available now!
