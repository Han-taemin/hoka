Information on backordered items

What if the item i want is not available? We make every effort to maintain an ample supply of our stock, but products may sell out or be discontinued.
  • Discontinued items will be removed from our website.
  • Orders can only be filled for items currently available on our website.
  • Items that are currently out of stock but are expected to be restocked will have a backorder date listed on the product page.
  • Backorder dates are the approximate date we are expecting inventory to arrive and are not guaranteed.
Can i be notified when it is back in stock? If the item you want is out of stock, you can sign up to be notified when it becomes available again:
  • Click on the item, color, and size you want.
  • Select the Join the Waitlist button to add your email to be notified.
Can i cancel my order if my item is backordered? Yes! If you would like to cancel your backordered item:
  • Click on the item, color, and size you want.
  • Select the Join the Waitlist button to add your email to be notified.
Can i cancel my order if my item is backordered? Yes! If you would like to cancel your backordered item:
  • Go to the Order Status page
  • Enter your order information (order number or email associated with the order, and billing zip code)
  • Select View Order Details
  • Locate the backordered item you wish to cancel, then click Cancel This Item

Once the item is canceled, the authorization of funds for the item amount will be released back to the original form of payment used on your order. Please note, although this is immediate on our end, it may take a few business days to reflect in your account based on your financial institution's policies.

When will i be charged for my backordered item(s)? Once your backordered item has shipped, your payment will be charged. You will also receive a shipping confirmation email complete with your tracking number to view the most up-to-date shipment progress via the carrier's website (UPS or FedEx depending on your region).

Until the backordered item ships, you will see an authorization pending for the backordered item amount. For reference, once your order is placed there is an authorization placed on your account for the total order amount. Your payment is not charged until your item(s) ship.

What communications should i expect/receive? Shortly after you place your order, you should receive an order confirmation email with your order details. Once the items from your order ship, you should receive a shipping confirmation email complete with tracking numbers to view the most up-to-date shipment progress via the carrier's website (UPS or FedEx depending on your region). You will also receive a delivery confirmation email.

We recommend keeping an eye on the carrier's website directly for the most accurate shipment details. You can also sign up to receive shipment updates, request delivery location changes, and customize some delivery options on their sites directly:
  • UPS (18-character tracking number, includes letters and numbers, typically beginning with 1Z): www.ups.com
  • FedEx (12-digit tracking number, all numerical): www.fedex.com

Why has my shipment been rescheduled? Due to global supply chain challenges, many products worldwide are experiencing transit delays. This, combined with the extreme increase in volume shipping carriers are experiencing has impacted the receipt of some items to our warehouse from what was originally planned.

All teams are working as quickly and as safely as possible to receive our inventory as originally intended. Once your item is available, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email complete with your tracking number to view the most up-to-date shipment progress via the carrier's website (UPS or FedEx depending on your region).

Will my items ship separately if only some are on backorder? Yes. We do our best to get items to you as quickly as possible, so if some items in your order are currently available, we will ship them out.

This could result in split shipments if some items in your order are on backorder. Once your backorder items become available, we will ship them out in a separate shipment. When the item is ready to send to you, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email complete with tracking numbers to view the most up-to-date shipment progress via the carrier's website.

Will any local retailers have the backordered item(s) available? Authorized retailers also carry select styles and colors. You can click HERE to find an authorized retailer near you, but please note that retail stores do not carry all lines, styles, or colors. It is recommended that you call prior to visiting.
