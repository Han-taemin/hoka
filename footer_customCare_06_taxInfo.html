Payment and tax information

In what currency are the prices on the website? Our prices are listed in us dollars (USD$).

Do You Price Match? HOKA does not price match with other retailers or websites. If you purchase an item directly from our website (HOKA.com) and the price decreases within 30 days of your purchase, we are happy to price match the item. Please contact our Customer Care team to see if your item is eligible:

Text/Call: 1-866-491-3125

Note: Price matches are eligible for items that have been marked down within 30 days of purchase. Must be same style, number, and color. Price matches are not eligible for items that are temporarily offered as part of a promotional event.

Do You Charge Sales Tax? Yes. Sales tax will be applied to online purchases made by customers in all states in accordance with state/city/county regulations. Sales tax is charged based on the shipping address of the order.

Sales tax is exempt in the following states:
  • Alaska
  • Delaware
  • Massachusetts (on clothing/footwear items less than $175)
  • Minnesota (clothing/footwear are exempt)
  • Montana
  • New Hampshire
  • New Jersey
  • New York (on clothing/footwear items less than $110 with exceptions*)
  • Oregon
  • Pennsylvania
  • Rhode Island (on clothing/footwear items less than $250)

Colorado retail delivery fee: As of July 1, 2022, the Colorado Department of Revenue requires all retail items shipped to a Colorado address using a motor vehicle for delivery (for example a UPS, FedEx, or USPS truck) to have an additional Retail Delivery Fee imposed on the order. This additional fee is only imposed on orders shipped to Colorado addresses. Deckers Brands will temporarily cover the cost of the newly imposed fee.

What methods of payment does hoka accept? We accept major credit cards including American Express, MasterCard, Visa, V.me by Visa, and Discover Card. We also accept payment through services such as PayPal, ApplePay, and Afterpay.

When using your credit card, please provide your credit card billing address if different from the shipping address and daytime/nighttime phone numbers. If the billing address or the security code does not match that which is on file at the financial institution, if authorization is not given for any reason, or if the issuing bank cannot support verification efforts, we reserve the right to cancel the order without notification. In most cases, "virtual credit cards" and prepaid cards cannot be verified. We do not recommend using a virtual credit card or prepaid cards on your HOKA orders. If you have any questions please call our customer service department at 1-866-491-3125.
