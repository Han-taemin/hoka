INNOVATION IS THE HEART OF HOKA

Three core technologies uniquely tuned in every shoe we make.

  1. Propulsion

    Engineered into the sole of our shoes, our curved Meta-Rocker keeps you from landing flat. Just like wheels for your feet, the curve helps propel people forward. Meta-Rocker also gives every stride the right boost of propulsion at just the right time, for a ride that's always smooth no matter the pace.

    Early Stage Meta-Rocker -Made to go fast, creates a smooth motion and faster transitions from heel to forefoot. Late Stage Meta-Rocker -Made for everyday, has the rocker point closer to the toe for added stability.

  2. Support

    Made to land each foot softly, then spring back up, the HOKA cushioned midsole acts as a high performance shock absorber without the weight. Dialed up or down, the plushness varies to deliver exact levels of performance and protection.

    Plush -Adds more comfort with higher bed of soft cushion. Provides a forgiving impact. Helps prevent or recover from injury with extra protection. Balanced -Provides a protective cushion. Maintains responsive toe-off to go faster. Responsive -Keeps foot closer to ground. Provides efficient push-off with firmer material. Delivers more bounce, more time in air, more speed.

  3. Stability

    The Active Foot Frame cradles your foot and secures your heel in place. Your foot becomes part of the shoe, instead of just resting on top. People describe it as feeling like their feet are being hugged by the shoe. The result is the ultimate support and confidence—regardless of what's underfoot.

    Rearfoot Active Foot Frame -Made for everyday, features our unique cradled experience in the rearfoot to support the heel. Midfoot Active Foot Frame -Made for faster paces, extends the foot frame forward to provide an enhanced cradled sensation when striking more mid-forefoot.
