How to return or exchange

To be eligible for exchange or return, your product must meet the following criteria:
  • Be an authentic HOKA product
  • Received from our site or another authorized online retailer within the last 30 days
  • Purchased at an authorized local retailer within the last 30 days
  • Be sent back as a complete pair

If your product does not meet all of the above criteria, it may still be eligible for a return under our warranty policy. Please refer to our warranty policy HERE.

Returning or exchanging products purchased from HOKA.com *Free returns and exchanges are eligible within the continental US only.

To start your return/exchange process, please click on the following link: HERE.

Returning or exchanging products purchased from HOKA.com *Free returns and exchanges are eligible within the continental US only.

To start your return/exchange process, please click on the following link: HERE.

Exchange requests are subject to product availability. If the requested item is no longer available at the time of return processing, a credit* will be issued.

*Credits provided will go back to the original form of payment for any online purchase. A site credit will be provided to you via email for use on our site for any items not purchased directly from HOKA.com.

Shipping costs will not be refunded upon return as they are one-time fees.

Afterpay Orders placed on hokaoneone.com using Afterpay as a form of payment can only be exchanged for the same model and color.

Does the original receipt need to be provided? We strongly encourage you to include a copy of your proof of purchase to confirm the product is within one year of the purchase date to be eligible under our warranty policy.

If returning an item for warranty evaluation, please note our warranty policy is only eligible on items within one year of the purchase date by the original customer. We strongly encourage you to include a copy of your proof of purchase to confirm the product is within one year of the purchase date to be eligible under our warranty policy. If you do not include your proof of purchase, your product will be evaluated by its manufacturing date. See Warranty Information for details.

Returning an item not purchased from HOKA.com We do accept returns from authorized HOKA retailers to potentially receive a credit for the value of the item. You can use the credit to replace the item at your convenience with a style of your choice available from our official site HOKA.com.

We cannot issue any refunds on shoes not originally purchased through our company. We sell our products at the suggested retail price and this is the amount of credit you will be issued. We are not responsible for additional markups incurred through the original purchase location.

*If you did not purchase your item on HOKA.com, you will be required to cover all return shipping costs to our warehouse. These costs are non-refundable. HOKA is not responsible for items lost in transit. We strongly recommend that you ship your package using a trackable shipping method. Please keep your tracking information until the return process is complete. We will require this information if you need to contact us regarding your return.

Once you have completed the form, you can print the return form and label directly from the confirmation page or through the link in the email confirmation we will send. Once your return or exchange has been processed, we will send you a confirmation email with details of your credit or exchange item's status.

Processing Timeframes: Most returns and exchanges process shortly after carrier scan, but certain items will need to be verified and processed by our warehouse team. Please allow up to 15 days after receipt at our warehouse for your return/exchange to process. Processing timeframes may vary based on volume. To track your return or exchange

HOKA HUB Some Fleet Feet locations in the US are able to help process exchanges and returns for purchases made directly from our HOKA site. Each of these participating Fleet Feet locations is known as a HOKA HUB. HOKA will notify eligible customers if they are near a HOKA HUB by notifying them in their Order Confirmation email, their Shipping Confirmation email, as well as at the beginning of the return process on our site's Return Center.

If you don't see any HOKA HUB information available regarding your order, then there is no HOKA HUB near you.
